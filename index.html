About the Property

This is a newly updated, walk-out, lakefront ranch home in Lake Arrowhead. The house is very close to snowmobile trails and many golf courses.

Lake arrowhead development offers a community swimming pool. Enjoy ice fishing and sledding down small hills to water in the winter or swimming and golfing in the summer. Our home is pet friendly with a pet deposit.

Features include 3 televisions with Satellite Dish, VCR and DVD player, fire place and fire pit outside. A washer, dryer and all linens are provided.

Keywords: Lake Arrowhead Home, snow, snowmobiling, lake property, lakefront, pets, pool, beautiful view.

About Teri Robertson

We are blessed to be able to rent it to your group. We have active teenage kids in too many sports. The ability to share our favorite place with you is a perfect fit for us. Some day when the kids move out, we will be able to use it more!

Teri Robertson purchased this cottage in 2006

Why Teri Robertson chose Gaylord

We are snowmobilers and we needed a place in the snow belt near the trails. This is perfect because we have kids and we dont have to ride on busy roads to get to the state trails. The local groomer is pretty visable. In the summer, we love to golf and this Gaylord location offers so many options in high quality golf courses. The kids like the community pool and lake fun. It offered the best of both worlds.

What makes this cottage unique

The view is great in every season. We have a view of the middle of the lake, but are protected by a treed island two houses to the left of our property. This protects our beach from jet ski's and creates a great cove for fishing.
